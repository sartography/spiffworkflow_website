It's about people, how they connect, and respecting their time.

Connect your teams with transparency and shared understanding. Glue your software systems together to minimize the noise. Then don't both people, until they need to be bothered.

Create Transparency Only when people understand processes will they can contribute to their improvement. Executable diagrams allow people to see what is happening behind the curtains. That transparency is two fold. It creates empathy (“I didn’t know you were handling so much!”) while also creating power (“Hey, if we reviewed this earlier, it would save everyone a lot of time”).

Automate The Bots No one wants 20 applications telling them what to do all day. In many cases, what is needed is a little glue – a way to pull information from one system, and use it in another. The Services Task in our diagrams allows us to talk to services, gather information, send them to other services, and automate ourselves out of the loop.

Deliver on Schedule We need more time by the fireplace for contempation, less time by the phone and it’s distractions. If we can reduce the noise, then we we can think in peace. Perhaps, maybe, do something of merit, of consequence. Something worthy.

Should we cancel school today?

Diagrams can be intutive AND executable - removing monotony, adding transpaency and opening opertunities for improvement and adaptation.
